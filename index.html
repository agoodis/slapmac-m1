The problem

Base M1 Macs have no accessible accelerometer

Classic slap apps rely on the IMU sensor — but it's not available on base M1 MacBooks. So if you have a MacBook Air M1 or base MacBook Pro M1... you're out of luck.

Our solution

We detect slaps through your microphone

A native Swift audio engine with high-pass filtering, adaptive noise floor, and impulse analysis. It distinguishes a slap from a clap, voice, or background noise.

More than just a slap app

Built from scratch as a native macOS app. Not a CLI tool. Not a Python script.

Mic-based detection

High-pass filter, peak/RMS analysis, crest-factor algorithm. Detects physical impact, ignores voice and noise.

Auto backend switching

Detects your hardware automatically. Uses IMU on M2+/M1 Pro, mic on base M1. Or pick manually.

Custom sound packs

Create your own packs with any sounds. Built-in and custom, normal and escalating modes. Open pack folders in Finder.

Dynamic volume

Hit harder — it gets louder. Volume scales with the force of your slap for a realistic response.

Fine-tuned controls

Sensitivity, cooldown, input gain, mic selection. Tune it for your specific MacBook and environment.

Native macOS app

Real SwiftUI interface. No terminal, no Python, no sudo. Just open and slap. Advanced settings and hardware diagnostics included.

FAQ

Will this damage my MacBook?

No more than any other slap app. The app only listens — it doesn't affect your hardware. But don't go full WWE on your laptop, obviously.

Which Macs are supported?

Any Apple Silicon Mac (M1, M1 Pro, M1 Max, M1 Ultra, M2, M2 Pro, M3, M4, etc.). Base M1 uses microphone detection, others can use either mic or IMU.

Does the mic mode pick up voices or music?

The detection algorithm uses high-pass filtering, adaptive noise floor, and crest-factor analysis specifically designed to distinguish physical impacts from voice, music, and ambient noise. You can also tune sensitivity and gain.

Is it really free?

Yes. No trials, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Just download and slap.

Can I add my own sounds?

Absolutely. Create custom sound packs, add any audio files, set them as normal or escalating. Manage everything from the app or open the pack folder directly in Finder.

Does it need internet access?

No. Everything runs locally. No data is sent anywhere.
